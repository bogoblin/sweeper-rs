InfiniteSweeper

Numbered tiles tell you how many mines are adjacent (orthogonally and diagonally) to the tile.

Right-click a hidden tile to put a flag on it, or remove an existing flag.

Click a hidden tile to reveal it - don't click on mines!

Press left click while holding right click to clear around a numbered tile if it has flags equal to its number surrounding it.

Click and drag to move around.
